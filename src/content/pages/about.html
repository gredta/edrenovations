About E&D Renovations

E&D Renovations is a licensed and insured general contracting company committed to delivering high-quality craftsmanship with honesty and professionalism. With years of experience in residential and commercial construction, we specialize in renovations, custom builds, and property improvements tailored to your needs.

What sets us apart is our team of specialized crews—each highly skilled in their trade. From expert flooring crews to professional painters, carpenters, and more, we bring in the right team for every phase of your project to ensure precision and efficiency.

Whether you're remodeling a kitchen, building an addition, or refreshing your interiors, we work with care, clear communication, and attention to detail to bring your vision to life—on time and within budget.

At E&D Renovations, your satisfaction is our top priority. From concept to completion, we’re here to build with integrity and deliver results that last.

DARDAN DULENIKU Founder & General Contractor
